Providing Sustainable Integrative Healthcare to Underserved Communities

Global Healthworks Foundation is an all-volunteer, charitable 501(c)(3) organization that provides sustainable integrative healthcare at no cost to underserved communities, both domestically and internationally. In the United States, our skilled practitioners serve in community-based outreach clinics providing treatments to war veterans, recovery patients and people living with HIV/AIDS (PLWHAs).

Internationally, our practitioners have treated communities in remote villages of Indonesia, Nepal and Guatemala. These communities face geographic, socio-economic, or political isolation and marginalization from healthcare services.

Typically, our healthcare services include acupuncture, Chinese herbal remedies, therapeutic soft-tissue bodywork, Reiki energy healing, and rehabilitative body movement exercises (e.g., qigong, yoga).

In addition to direct patient care, Global Healthworks Foundation works to train local health advocates in the communities we serve to empower their communities with tools for sustainable health.
